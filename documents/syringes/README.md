# Syringes

### Silicone Glue

The silicone glue syringe is filled with [Tec7 Rock](https://tec7.com/en-BE/products/mount-seal/rock). Technical and Safety Datasheet can be found on the website. A copy of the safety datasheet is located in the["DS"](DS) folder.

### Silicone Grease
The silicone grease syringe is filled with [weicon silicone grease](https://www.weicon.de/en/silicone-grease-food-safe-lubricating-grease/10050566). Technical and Safety Datasheet can be found on the website. A copy of the safety datasheet is located in the["DS"](DS) folder.
