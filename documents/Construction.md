## 🔧 How to build OpenValve

OpenValve is not available as a ready-to-use consumer product yet and must be self-assembled.

### To build your own you need:
- [the OpenValve assembly kit + a soil moisture sensor](https://lectronz.com/products/openvalve-assembly-kit-v1-0)
- an enclosed 3D printer* (capable of printing ASA without warping)
- ASA filament*
- 1 x 9V Alkaline Battery
- solder equipment
- basic hand tools (allen keys, pliers)

*Optionally all missing 3D-printed parts can be purchased with the assembly kit, in which case there is no 3D printer or filament necessary to build OpenValve.


### Assembly Guide
If you bought an OpenValve assembly kit and want assemble it: Watch the [Assembly Guide Video](https://youtu.be/af_efyuOWUs). The video guides through the assembly with detailed step-by-step assembly instructions.
